Password Best Practices

Make it unique

Use passwords unique to different online accounts. This reduces the likelihood that one compromised password would impact other accounts.

❌ DON'T use the same password for every account.

✅ DO use passwords unique to every account.

Make it random

Ensure your passwords do not follow a recognizable pattern and use combinations of letters, numbers, and symbols.

❌ DON'T use passwords related to personal information.

✅ DO use random combinations of characters.

Make it long

An 8-character password takes hours to crack, while 16 characters can take billions of years.

❌ DON'T use fewer than 14 characters.

✅ DO use 14 characters or more.
